Tether-Assisted Spacelaunch Systems

About

Space advancement and exploration is halted by the difficulties and expenses imposed by traditional rocket spacelaunch systems. For traveling sustainably beyond geostationary orbit and utilising lunar or martian resources, a radical innovation should be implemented unto the launch act itself. This is why we have set our sights on tensile structures that will assist spacecrafts and payloads on their way to space. The maths have been done, and the theories checks out. It is time to test them in practice.
