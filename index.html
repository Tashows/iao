Tether-Assisted Spacelaunch Systems

Aim

Space advancement and exploration is halted by the difficulties and expenses imposed by traditional rocket spacelaunch systems. For traveling sustainably, beyond geostationary orbit, and utilising lunar or martian resources, a radical innovation should be implemented unto the launch act itself. This is why we have set our sights on tensile structures that will assist spacecrafts and payloads on their way to space.

We are working with specific scientific models to bring the project one step closer to reality.

IAO acts as a networking platform for aerospace-oriented individuals interested in the research and development of tether-assisted spacelaunch systems.

IAO plans to connect passionate researchers through events and meetings, which aim to foster a collaboration on a professional level.

IAO is characterized as a company in the making, since it seeks to attract investors and granting organizations for further expansion.
